6. Booking Process

How Bookings Work:

  • Customers select services, location (area and city), and preferred time

  • Professionals have a stipulated time to accept the job
  • Once accepted, both parties can communicate via in-app chat
  • Customer pays the remaining amount upon completion of the booked service

The Platform facilitates connections but does not guarantee service quality or completion.
